About VISA

Attendees from most countries will be required to obtain a visa to enter the People's Republic of China; Attendees can apply for a Chinese visa through a travel agency or directly at the nearest Chinese embassy.

You may need an invitation letter to get a visa in the nearest Chinese Embassy/Consulate.
To get the invitation letter, please contact tase2016@sei.ecnu.edu.cn and provide:

  • Full Name (exactly as in your passport)
  • Passport Number
  • Your Nationality
  • The dates you expect to arrive and depart China

We will email you the signed letter which will state the conference name, the duration of the conference, your detailed correspondence that will be extracted from your enquiry.
